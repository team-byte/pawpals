Our Mission

Here at the PawPals team we strive to connect people and pets for socialization and social networking in a positive and trusting matter.

By Providing Pals with the ability to connect with other Pals we drive a sense of community and connectivity In the modern world our pets are with us day to day. Some animals such as dogs live much better lives through proper socialization and we strive to deliver that opportunity to our pals.

As a growing community PawPals will strive to deliver the experience and opportunity for our Pals to connect, leave reviews and in the future, schedule and request playdates with other Pals and their animals.
